Dianne!

Dianne moved to Portland from Albuquerque, New Mexico in 1996. After a brief stint at a local salon, she opened Halo. Dianne has frequently traveled to New York and San Francisco to attend hands on classes on razor cutting, color and styling. Her commitment to education is passed on to her stylists as well. With over 30 years behind the chair, she is confident in color and the use of a razor. She specializes in corrective color and Asian hair cutting.


Jessica!

Jessica loves all things “Hair”.She knew this by age 10, when she began cutting notonly baby dolls and barbie dolls hair, but her younger sister’s hair as well. Because of her love to work with all kinds of hair types she has developed a highly diverse clientele, and enjoys each and every one of them. If you are maintaining and refreshing your current look, or creating a whole new one, Jessica would like to invite you to become an important part of her amazing clientele.


Lauren!

Lauren has been in this industry since 2006, with a focus on hair for over six years.She specializes in haircutting and coloring for men and women, as well as event hairstyling, fusion bond hair extensions, and keratin infusion smoothing treatments. She consistently strives to create a memorable experience for her clients, starting with an in-depth consultation to achieve the best style combination to fit their lifestyle and maintenance routines.


Sydney!

Sydney has been with Halo Salon since 2001. She has 28 years of experience to her profession. Sydney’s has been an on-going educating journey which includes Goldwell color, Bumble & bumble product knowledge and Bumble & bumble cutting classes. She loves creating natural looking hair color for all of her clients. Her method for hair dressing starts with precision, and for her, “doing hair is creative, fun, & social.


Casey!

She draws her inspiration from art, history, music and fashion to keep her ideas fresh.If you to refreshing your current look, Jessica is right person for you!


Jenica!
